Website Evaluations

Palantir

This is Palantir's main page. Palantir has a very nice format that really appeals to the user. With a simple navigation at the top with some text. As you scroll you see more and more images and description of their company.

  • Contrast - This page contains very nice contract with the texting being easy to read. The black and white color skem really helps!
  • Repetition - This site has very nice repetition. Repeating elements and section! This makes everything very nice and confortable for the user.
  • Alignment - The page is aligned consistently throughout the entirety. Each section hold the text aligned in the same way and this creates a great user experience.
  • Proximity - All the related elements are grouped together in a section. And all elements that are separated by a dash are talking about different topics.

Yale School Of Arts

The Yale School of Arts is the arts page of yale. Hosting an abudance of information about the page. However, the page is using a multitude of color seems that are not consistent and appealing to the user!

  • Contrast - The Background has a black color skem but another blue mini background that seems out of place. This followed by a gradient color skem sections with white and yellow. This contrast doesn't look nice and seems out of place.
  • Repetition - There are section of boxed items. However, there doesn't seem to be a pattern to them. The page seems to have different boxed items for each area. Until the end, this is where they keep the section with the gradient color skem.
  • Alignment - The alignment at the beginning doesn't match the alignment at the end. This is clear beacause the alignment at the beginning has items that are on centered. On the other hand, at the bottom, the items and boxes are completely centered.
  • Proximity - They try to create nice proximity with a dark blue background, however, it doesn't turn out the best with that background randomly ending in the middle of sections where it should be together.
